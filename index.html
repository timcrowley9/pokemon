The Procedure

A unique QR code is mailed to a registered voter upon request. On election day, a voter enters an email address and password. The voter is then directed to a QR scanner which takes in their unique code. After answering a few simple security questions the user may vote in a safe, secure and convenient way.

Get Your Voting Code in the Mail

Voters will have a QR code mailed to them during election season. Once the user receives the letter they will be prompted to enter a valid email and password online to ensure a user's identity is confirmed and no fraud is committed.

  

Scan Your Code with our PokemonGOtothePolls App

Once a user has their identity confirmed, they will scan the QR code recieved in their letter. After scanning the user will be brought to their mobile ballot in a secure easily accessible manner.
